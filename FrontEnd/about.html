About PulmoScan

PulmoScan is an AI-powered platform designed to assist in early lung disease detection through advanced medical image analysis.

Our Mission

Our mission is to combine artificial intelligence with medical imaging to help detect lung diseases earlier and improve healthcare outcomes. By using deep learning models trained on CT scan data, PulmoScan provides quick and reliable analysis for healthcare professionals and researchers.
